## How to Sign

When you open a pull request against the IronForge repository, the CLA
Assistant bot will post a comment asking you to confirm that you have
read and agreed to this Agreement. Reply to that comment with exactly:

> `I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA`

Your signature is recorded against your GitHub handle and the commit
SHA. You only need to sign once per handle.

If your contribution is made **on behalf of a company**, please email
[licensing@structupath.ai](mailto:licensing@structupath.ai) before
opening the PR so we can execute a Corporate CLA.
