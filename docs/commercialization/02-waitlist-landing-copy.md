## SECTION 1 — "THE PROBLEM WE'RE SOLVING"

### H2
The system is designed to keep you on the tools.

### Body
Every journeyman who wants to run their own shop hits the same wall: a
thousand-page pile of state forms, bond quotes, workers' comp classifications,
NAICS codes, capability statements, and signatory paperwork that nobody
teaches you at the hall. The info exists — it's just scattered across 50
Secretary of State websites, 12 federal portals, 200+ local unions, and a
handful of $400/hr attorneys.

So most guys either:
- **Give up** and stay on someone else's payroll,
- **Go non-union** because it's "easier," draining the trade, or
- **Quit mid-process** after six months of spinning their wheels.

IronForge exists because the trade can't afford any of those outcomes.

## SECTION 2 — "WHAT IRONFORGE DOES"

### H2
A guided wizard, a regulatory engine, and an AI mentor that knows the trade.

### 3-column feature grid (neon-bordered cards)

**▌ 50-STATE CONTENT ENGINE**
Filing fees. Bond amounts. Workers' comp type (monopolistic vs. competitive
vs. private). Prevailing wage thresholds. MBE/WBE/DBE programs. Updated by
humans, verified against agency URLs — not scraped.

**▌ 7-PHASE WIZARD**
Business Formation → Licensing → Bonding → Insurance → Certifications →
Union Signatory → Legal & Federal. Every step has a checklist, cost estimate,
timeline, and direct link to the agency form you actually need.

**▌ AI MENTOR (CLAUDE)**
Streaming chat scoped to your current phase, step, state, and profile. It
won't hallucinate a fake bond agent — system prompts are grounded in the
state registry and refuse out-of-scope questions.

**▌ UNION SIGNATORY MODULE**
Every Ironworkers local in the U.S. — local number, hall phone, jurisdiction,
district council, trust fund breakdown, CBA walkthrough, signing procedure.
Built with district council input.

**▌ CERTIFICATION PATHWAYS**
SAM.gov, SDVOSB, 8(a), HUBZone, DBE/MBE/WBE. Profile flags (veteran,
disabled-vet, minority, woman-owned) surface only the programs you qualify
for.

**▌ CAPABILITY STATEMENT GENERATOR**
Fills in NAICS, past projects, differentiators, certifications. Exports to
a bid-ready one-pager.

## SECTION 3 — "HOW IT WORKS"

### H2
Three steps. No guesswork.

**01 · PICK YOUR STATE**
Tap your state on the map. Flag veteran / minority / woman-owned status.
The platform pre-loads every requirement specific to your jurisdiction.

**02 · WALK THE PHASES**
Move through the seven phases at your own pace. Check boxes as you go. Ask
the mentor anything. Your progress persists — locally now, in your account
at launch.

**03 · GO SIGNATORY. GO TO WORK.**
Export your capability statement, sign with your local, and land your first
bid. Average user goes from "thinking about it" to signatory in 60–90 days
versus the industry norm of 12–18 months.
